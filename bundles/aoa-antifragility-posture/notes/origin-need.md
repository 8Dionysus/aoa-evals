# Origin Need

The first-wave antifragility landing needed one bounded proof surface that could answer a narrow question:

did one owner-local surface handle one named stressor family in a bounded, source-owned, anti-widening way?

That question sits between:
- repo-local receipts
- broader workflow or artifact evals
- derived stats summaries

`aoa-antifragility-posture` exists to keep that middle proof layer explicit without collapsing the ownership split.
