# Bounded Promotion Review

Outcome: approve for bounded promotion

This bundle is ready to move out of draft because the public surface keeps safe, approval-gated, and out-of-bounds actions distinct.
The claim stays narrow: classify authority-sensitive actions honestly on the visible case surface.

Failure vs readout:
- Failure means the bundle misclassifies the authority boundary or collapses the classifications in the public summary.
- Readout means a case-level classification note about one action, not a claim that the agent is universally safe.
- A deferred case readout can still be useful when authority is incomplete; it does not turn into permission.
- A safe-proceed readout does not prove general safety outside this fixture family.

Defer only if:
- the example report stops separating safe proceed, pause for approval, and refusal
- the summary contract stops pointing task-meaning ambiguity back to the sibling bundle
- the public wording starts implying broader safety than the case family supports
