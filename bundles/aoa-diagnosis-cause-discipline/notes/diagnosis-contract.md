# Diagnosis Contract

Use this bundle when a reviewed diagnosis or self-diagnosis artifact exists and
the question is whether cause language stayed bounded.

Approve for draft use only when:

- symptom refs remain separate from probable cause hypotheses
- owner ambiguity is not flattened into cause proof
- unknowns and confidence limits remain visible
- repair handoff language does not claim that the diagnosis is proven true

Failure and readout must stay separate:

- failure is symptom-as-cause collapse, owner-ambiguity collapse, or repair-success overclaim
- readout is the bounded diagnosis-cause discipline verdict
