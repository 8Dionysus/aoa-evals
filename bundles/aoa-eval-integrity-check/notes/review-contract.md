# Review Contract

Use this bundle to review one current public starter bundle at a time.

approve for bounded promotion
defer for now when the integrity surface is still too structural or too easy to overread
failure is semantic overreach, thin evidence, bundle blur, or routing drift
readout is the bounded public wording that reports that integrity risk

Required target dossier:
- `EVAL.md`
- `eval.yaml`
- `examples/example-report.md`
- `reports/example-report.json` when a bundle ships a schema-backed report artifact
- support notes and checks
- `EVAL_INDEX.md` starter row and distinctness note
- `EVAL_SELECTION.md` posture and chooser wording

Required review classes:
- manifest-to-EVAL mismatch
- verdict-shape or report-shape mismatch
- category or routing mismatch
- blind-spot or nearby-bundle distinctness weakness
- baseline or comparative semantics mismatch where applicable
- weak evidence or support coverage
- style-over-substance failure
- baseline by association
- artifact/process collapse
- comparative overread
- growth overclaiming

The resulting note should name one integrity risk class directly.
If the dossier is mixed,
prefer `mixed support` over a cleaner-looking pass.
