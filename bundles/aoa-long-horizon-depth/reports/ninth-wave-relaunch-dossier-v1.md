## Simulated relaunch route

1. `router` triages the relaunch as a bounded restart-fidelity question and routes to the smallest next planning surface.
2. `planner` shapes a bounded pass around checkpoint review, contradiction carry-forward, and next-test continuity.
3. `executor` performs the relaunch from portable artifacts rather than from raw session history.
4. `verifier` checks contradiction survival, memo-versus-canon separation, and restart-boundary honesty.
5. `conductor` decides whether the next move is continue, deepen, pause, or distill.
6. `archivist` prepares any post-pass writeback candidates without collapsing memo state into canon.

This route stays consistent with:

- `task_to_tier_hints.json` as the thin routing hint surface
- `model_tier_registry.json` as the source-owned tier posture
- `restartable-inquiry-loop` as the scenario-level handoff and artifact contract
