# Origin Need

`aoa-evals` already has:
- one-run workflow starters
- same-task frozen-baseline comparison
- artifact-versus-process bridge surfaces

What it still lacked was a bounded repeated-window movement surface.

`aoa-longitudinal-growth-snapshot` exists to answer a modest question:
does the same bounded workflow surface look stronger, weaker, flat, or unstable across ordered windows?

This bundle stays report-led on purpose.
It does not introduce raw run-history infrastructure,
and it does not turn repeated-window movement into a claim of broad capability growth.
