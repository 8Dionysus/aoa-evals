# Revision Window Contract

Use this bundle when a route already names a bounded `revision_window_ref` and
the question is whether reflective revision stayed inside it.

Approve for draft use only when:

- the revision window is explicit
- stop-lines remain visible during revision
- widen-by-convenience failures are still representable
- reanchor or defer remains possible before the route loses boundedness

Failure and readout must stay separate:

- failure is hidden widening, erased stop-lines, or revision without a real boundary
- readout is the bounded reflective-revision-boundedness verdict
