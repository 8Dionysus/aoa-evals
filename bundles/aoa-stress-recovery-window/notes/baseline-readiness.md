# Baseline Readiness

This bundle uses `longitudinal-window`,
so its readiness claim is about comparable ordered stress recovery windows rather than one frozen baseline.

Minimum readiness for this surface:

- the windows are named and ordered before longitudinal reading begins
- one named owner surface or tightly related surface family stays in scope
- the same named stressor family remains visible across the window
- source-owned owner receipts remain the strongest evidence
- route hints and memo context stay secondary
- the summary can distinguish:
  - healthy bounded movement
  - mixed movement
  - fragile movement
  - suppressed readout when evidence is too thin
- the support notes remain aligned with `reports/example-report.json` and `reports/stress-recovery-window-proof-flow-v1.md`

If those conditions do not hold,
the honest result is not a stronger repeated-window claim.
