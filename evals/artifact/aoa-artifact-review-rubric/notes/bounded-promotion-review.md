# Bounded Promotion Review

This note is the `support_note` for moving `aoa-artifact-review-rubric` from `draft` to `bounded`.

Outcome: approve for bounded promotion

Approve for bounded promotion when:
- the bundle stays on produced artifact quality rather than workflow discipline
- rubric-axis notes remain visible instead of collapsing into polish theater
- the public readout keeps task fit, coherence, completeness, and reviewability legible
- the summary does not upgrade strong artifact presentation into workflow proof

Defer bounded promotion when:
- polish is used to hide visible bounded defects or missing requirements
- rubric axes collapse into one unreviewable aesthetic score
- the public wording treats artifact strength as proof of process discipline
- hidden local standards are required to explain the readout

Failure vs readout:
- failure is the mismatch between the artifact claim and the visible artifact evidence
- readout is the public summary of that mismatch
- a clean readout cannot repair missed visible requirements
- a clumsy readout does not by itself invalidate a supported artifact judgment
