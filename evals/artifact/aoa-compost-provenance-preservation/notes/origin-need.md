# Origin Need

This starter bundle exists because witness-derived notes can look elegant while quietly severing their provenance.

The public pilot pair needs a bounded proof surface that checks whether:
- source refs survive distillation
- review state remains visible
- limits remain named
- principle candidates do not masquerade as canon
- contradiction,
  stale posture,
  or later demotion remain possible

Without that surface,
the compost route risks becoming a philosophy-shaped summary generator rather than a provenance-preserving digestion path.
