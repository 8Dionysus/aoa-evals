# Proof Boundary

This bundle can support a bounded read that one supplied recurrence dossier
kept the control-plane posture intact on the observed axes.

It cannot support a claim that recurrence is complete, that owner artifacts are
ready for promotion, that a beacon is a verdict, or that routing, stats, KAG,
or Agon surfaces gained source authority.

Missing axes remain `not_observed`, not passes. A supported read should still
travel with owner-specific evals when the question moves from recurrence
boundary integrity to artifact quality, source truth, or promotion readiness.
