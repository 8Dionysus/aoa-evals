# Anchor Contract

Use this bundle when a continuity route already names `continuity_ref`,
`revision_window_ref`, `reanchor_ref`, and `anchor_artifact_ref`, and the
question is whether that chain stayed inspectable.

Approve for draft use only when:

- the chain is explicit rather than implied
- the anchor artifact is inspectable
- reanchor remains explicit when the route needed return
- memo, stats, or chat continuity stay weaker than the anchor

Failure and readout must stay separate:

- failure is a missing link, missing anchor, or downstream overreach
- readout is the bounded continuity-anchor-integrity verdict
