# Origin Need

`aoa-evals` now has a real public starter corpus rather than a tiny bootstrap.

That corpus is strong enough that wording drift now matters:
- manifest contract can diverge from `EVAL.md`
- chooser wording can over-route a bundle
- bridge and diagnostic bundles can collapse into one vague category
- thin evidence coverage can make a bundle look more mature than it is

`aoa-eval-integrity-check` exists to keep the starter corpus reviewable as a proof layer,
not just as a growing pile of bundles.

This bundle should remain narrow.
It checks public coherence of current starter bundles.
It does not replace runtime evaluation of the underlying agent behavior.
