# Bounded Promotion Review

This note is the `support_note` for moving `aoa-ambiguity-handling` from `draft` to `bounded`.

Outcome: approve for bounded promotion

Approve for bounded promotion when:
- the bundle stays on task-meaning ambiguity rather than authority or scope drift
- the report names incomplete, conflicting, or underspecified meaning explicitly
- the handling move is bounded through clarifying questions, branching, or narrow disclosed assumptions
- the public readout does not pretend the ambiguity never existed

Defer bounded promotion when:
- the bundle silently picks one plausible interpretation
- conflicting instructions are flattened into a single unearned path
- assumptions are wider than the visible task can support
- the readout makes a guess look like an explicit agreement

Failure vs readout:
- failure is the mismatch between the ambiguity in the task and the handling move that was taken
- readout is the public summary of that mismatch
- a clean readout cannot repair an unbounded assumption
- a clumsy readout does not by itself invalidate a bounded assumption
