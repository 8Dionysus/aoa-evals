# Agon / Epistemic Alignment Part

## Role

This part owns epistemic alignment surfaces that test candidate proof posture
without live judgment, doctrine rewrite, or owner-truth takeover.

It bounds epistemic pressure before it can masquerade as accepted truth.

## Route

`epistemic pressure -> alignment seed -> generated registry -> candidate-only boundary validation`

## Inputs

- epistemic pressure records, owner-boundary refs, and forbidden effects;
- candidate-only status, runtime-effect fields, and required stop-lines;
- part-local docs, schema, example, builder, validator, tests, and observe-only
  recurrence bindings.

## Outputs

- deterministic `agon_epistemic_eval_alignment_registry.min.json` output;
- candidate-only epistemic boundary checks;
- observe-only recurrence component metadata when present;
- validation failures when a record implies live judgment, doctrine rewrite, or
  owner-truth takeover.

## Stronger Owner Split

`aoa-evals` owns local epistemic alignment checks and generated candidate
navigation.

Agents-of-Abyss, Tree of Sophia, KAG, memo, stats, and runtime owners keep
truth authority, doctrine, canon, and live judgment.

## Stop-Lines

- Do not rewrite doctrine, decide truth, promote to Tree of Sophia, or take over
  owner truth.
- Do not issue live verdict, durable scar, memory write, retention execution,
  rank mutation, or hidden scheduler action.
- Do not let generated epistemic records outrank source owners or bundle-local
  review.
