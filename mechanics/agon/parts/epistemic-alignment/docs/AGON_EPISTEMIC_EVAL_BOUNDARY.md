# Agon Epistemic Eval Boundary

## Role

This note is the boundary companion for epistemic alignment.

It states what `aoa-evals` may inspect when epistemic pressure appears in Agon
work, and where the stronger owner line remains.

## Inspectable Pressure

Candidate checks may assess:

- prediction accuracy;
- concept-delta legitimacy;
- bifurcation legitimacy;
- rupture candidates;
- source-owner refs and missing evidence;
- overclaim risk in proposed epistemic wording.

## Boundary

These checks make epistemic pressure reviewable. They do not settle truth,
rewrite doctrine, promote canon, issue live verdicts, or turn generated registry
output into owner acceptance.

## Owner Route

Start at `mechanics/agon/parts/epistemic-alignment/README.md`, then route from
the seed and registry back to the owner refs named by the record.

## Validation

Use the part `VALIDATION.md` route and parent `parts/AGENTS.md` child validation
block. A passing registry proves shape and boundary discipline; it does not
prove the epistemic claim itself.
