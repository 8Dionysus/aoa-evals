# KAG Eval Boundary

## Role

This boundary note keeps KAG alignment from being over-read as owner acceptance.

It is the short read to open when a generated KAG alignment record looks
stronger than a candidate check.

## Inspectable Pressure

`aoa-evals` may inspect:

- candidate legitimacy;
- pattern or repetition floors;
- retention evidence floors;
- owner-boundary refs;
- demotion, quarantine, and promotion pressure;
- Tree of Sophia boundary risk.

## Boundary

Eval alignment can identify whether a proposed KAG-facing proof surface is
bounded enough for review. It cannot accept a KAG object, canonize a substrate,
mutate rank, execute retention, or promote material to Tree of Sophia.

## Route

Start with `mechanics/agon/parts/kag-alignment/README.md`. Use the seed,
schema, and generated registry as local support surfaces, then return to the KAG
or ToS owner for acceptance questions.

## Validation

Use the part `VALIDATION.md` route and parent `parts/AGENTS.md` child validation
block. Passing validation preserves the candidate-only boundary; it does not
grant owner truth.
