# Schools / Lineages / Campaigns Eval Alignment

## Role

This source note names the eval-side route for Schools / Lineages / Campaigns
alignment pressure.

It lets `aoa-evals` check whether SLC candidates are bounded enough for review
without moving school, lineage, campaign, or canon authority into this repo.

## Route

```text
SLC pressure
-> alignment seed
-> generated registry
-> non-canon validation
```

## Reads

Use this surface for candidate school, lineage, campaign, rivalry, branch
survival, and doctrine-evolution checks.

## Boundary

`aoa-evals` owns candidate SLC alignment and local validation.

The owners of schools, lineages, campaigns, Tree of Sophia canon, and
Agents-of-Abyss law keep source truth and promotion authority.

## Validation

Follow the commands in `mechanics/agon/parts/slc-alignment/README.md`.

Registry success means candidate shape and stop-line discipline survived. It is
not school authority, campaign authority, owner acceptance, or canon authority.
