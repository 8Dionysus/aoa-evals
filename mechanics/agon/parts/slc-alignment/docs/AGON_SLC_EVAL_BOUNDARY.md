# SLC Eval Boundary

## Role

This boundary note prevents SLC eval alignment from being read as live school,
lineage, campaign, or canon authority.

## Inspectable Pressure

`aoa-evals` may inspect candidate quality around:

- school legitimacy;
- lineage continuity;
- campaign survival;
- rivalry or branch evidence;
- doctrine-evolution claims;
- owner-boundary refs.

## Boundary

The alignment can expose candidate quality and overclaim risk. It cannot issue
verdict sovereignty, grant closure, write memory, mutate rank, promote KAG
material, or promote Tree of Sophia canon.

## Route

Start from `mechanics/agon/parts/slc-alignment/README.md`. Use the seed,
schema, and registry as local support surfaces, then route acceptance or canon
questions to the stronger owner.

## Validation

Use the part `VALIDATION.md` route and parent `parts/AGENTS.md` child validation
block. Passing validation keeps this as a candidate-only proof route, not owner
acceptance.
