# Antifragility / Part Index

`mechanics/antifragility/parts/` contains the active parts of the eval-side
antifragility proof operation.

The mechanic owns the route:

`antifragility pressure -> bounded stress, recovery, or repair-proof question -> selected evidence -> bundle-local review -> bounded report or owner handoff`

## Parts

| Part | Role | Active surfaces |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `posture-review` | Maintains first-wave owner-local antifragility posture proof support. | `mechanics/antifragility/parts/posture-review/README.md` |
| `stress-recovery-window` | Maintains repeated-window stress recovery support while comparison readout stays in `comparison-spine`. | `mechanics/antifragility/parts/stress-recovery-window/README.md` |
| `repair-proof` | Maintains bounded repair-proof support while repair authority and growth-cycle doctrine stay with their owners. | `mechanics/antifragility/parts/repair-proof/README.md` |

## Part Contract

Inputs are owner-local stressor receipts, degraded continuation or safe-stop
evidence, ordered stress recovery windows, selected runtime evidence packets,
repair or reanchor artifact trails, and bundle-local report contracts.

Outputs are bounded antifragility posture reads, longitudinal stress recovery
reads, bounded repair-proof notes, explicit blind spots, suppressed or mixed
postures when owner evidence is thin, and owner handoff routes.

Owner split stays explicit: `Agents-of-Abyss` owns antifragility law; runtime,
memory, stats, routing, playbook, KAG, and owner repositories keep their local
truth; `aoa-evals` owns proof wording, verdict logic, repair proof, regression
evidence, and report interpretation.

Stop-lines route stronger-owner pressure through the boundary routes below.

| Pressure | Owner route |
| --- | --- |
| global resilience or federation health | `Agents-of-Abyss` doctrine route plus source-owner evidence review |
| one-score health or antifragility movement | `aoa-stats` vector-window route plus AoA doctrine review |
| deletion theater, cleanup authority, runtime self-healing, or owner-local repair execution | owner repository or `abyss-stack` runtime route |
| route, memo, stats, KAG, playbook, or generated-reader truth promotion | owning route, memo, stats, KAG, playbook, generated-source, and owner-receipt routes |
| diagnosis-cause discipline or growth-cycle movement | `mechanics/growth-cycle/parts/diagnosis-gate/` route |

Validation is the affected bundle validation, generated catalog check, root
repository validation, and the antifragility mechanic route checks in
`scripts/validate_repo.py`.

## Routed Neighbor Families

`aoa-diagnosis-cause-discipline` routes through
`mechanics/growth-cycle/parts/diagnosis-gate/`. It is already active there as
the Growth Cycle diagnosis gate; `repair-proof`, any proposed `repair` parent
shape, and Antifragility proximity all route back to that owner before any
future evidence pass changes the topology.

Any future antifragility part still needs a separate evidence pass that names
its own operation, inputs, outputs, owner split, stop-lines, and validation.
