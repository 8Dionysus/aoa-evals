# Distillation Direction

Distillation in `aoa-evals` should make provenance-preserving abstraction and
reviewed runtime-candidate adoption testable without turning summaries into
truth.

Use this file for the package's current operating direction: read it after the
parent entry card and before `PARTS.md`, part contracts, source bundles,
decision records, and `PROVENANCE.md`.

## Source-of-truth split

- `README.md`: package entry card and shortest proof-side route.
- `DIRECTION.md`: current operating direction.
- `PARTS.md`: active distillation part map.
- `parts/`: compost provenance and runtime-candidate adoption support.
- `PROVENANCE.md`: controlled bridge from active route to old distillation-adjacent placement.
- `legacy/`: lineage only; not a summary archive.
- `evals/`: source proof objects that remain stronger than part support.

## Current contour

- Keep the parent name `distillation`; compost provenance and reviewed
  candidate adoption are parts.
- Keep source traces, raw material, and candidate lineage visible.
- Keep runtime-pack hook metadata under `audit`.
- Keep generic Experience adoption under `experience`.
- Keep memo recall under `recurrence/memory-recall`.

## Growth rule

Add a distillation part only when a repeated abstraction or adoption operation
needs a proof-side support surface. Do not move contradiction, base writeback,
witness trace, or generic adoption work here without a separate evidence pass.

## Stop-lines

- Do not claim ToS canon, memory canon, KAG lift, runtime activation, owner
  acceptance, or bridge-ready truth.
- Do not let summary replace source trace or reviewed candidate evidence.
