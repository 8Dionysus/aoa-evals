# Distillation / Compost Provenance Part

## Role

This part owns support surfaces for provenance-preserving compost proof.

It keeps witness-derived input, downstream note or principle candidate,
source refs, review state, limits, promotion boundary, and contradiction or
demotion posture visible while source proof bundles stay under `evals/`.

## Source Surfaces

- `evals/artifact/aoa-compost-provenance-preservation/EVAL.md`
- `mechanics/distillation/parts/compost-provenance/fixtures/compost-provenance-v1/README.md`

## Inputs

- witness-facing input such as `WitnessTrace` or `witness_summary_md`;
- compost note, synthesis note, principle candidate, or canon-facing candidate;
- source refs, review state, current limits, contradiction or demotion posture;
- bundle-local fixture, runner, and report contracts.

## Outputs

- bounded provenance-preservation proof reading;
- fixture family route for compost provenance cases;
- report expectation for source-ref retention, review-state visibility,
  limit honesty, promotion boundary, and contradiction or decay posture;
- owner handoff route when compost meaning, principle truth, canon posture, or
  witness truth belongs to a stronger owner.

## Stronger Owner Split

`Agents-of-Abyss` owns Distillation law and provenance bridge vocabulary.
Tree-of-Sophia owns authored meaning, compost canon, principle truth, and
canon-facing interpretation; this includes Tree-of-Sophia meaning. The owner
repository owns the original run quality and source artifact context.
`aoa-memo` owns memory-object truth when a compost artifact becomes memory.
`comparison-spine` owns artifact/process comparison when comparison is the
claim.

`aoa-evals` owns this part's bounded provenance-preservation proof wording,
fixture support, report expectations, and bundle-local interpretation for
`aoa-compost-provenance-preservation`. Authority beyond those proof readings
routes through the stronger owner split above.
