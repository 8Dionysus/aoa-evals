# Method-growth / Part Index

`mechanics/method-growth/parts/` contains the active parts of the eval-side
method-growth proof operation.

The mechanic owns the route:

`growth-refinery pressure -> lineage or owner-landing proof question -> selected proof artifacts -> bundle-local review -> bounded verdict or owner handoff`

## Parts

| Part | Role | Active surfaces |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `candidate-lineage` | Maintains lineage-chain coherence support across checkpoint carry, reviewed candidate, seed staging, owner evidence, dropped branches, and supersession. | `mechanics/method-growth/parts/candidate-lineage/README.md` |
| `owner-landing` | Maintains owner-fit routing support for reviewed growth-refinery candidates without replacing final owner acceptance. | `mechanics/method-growth/parts/owner-landing/README.md` |

## Part Contract

Inputs are checkpoint carry, reviewed candidate, staged seed, downstream owner
evidence, dropped or superseded branches, reviewed owner hypotheses, nearest
wrong targets, and derivative-repo exclusion evidence.

Outputs are bounded lineage-integrity verdicts, bounded owner-fit routing
reads, explicit missing-stage or overclaim flags, nearest-wrong-target notes,
derivative-drift notes, and owner handoff routes.

Owner split stays explicit: `Agents-of-Abyss` owns Method-growth law and route
grammar; final owner repositories own final object truth; `aoa-skills`,
`Dionysus`, `aoa-techniques`, `aoa-playbooks`, `aoa-memo`, and `aoa-stats` own
their local candidate, seed, technique, method, memory, and summary truths;
`aoa-evals` owns proof wording, verdict logic, fixture/report contracts, and
claim limits.

Stop-lines forbid final owner-object quality claims, owner-local activation,
lineage-as-owner-fit proof, owner-fit-as-final-quality proof, derivative
first-authoring drift, universal growth scores, diagnosis-cause ownership,
repair success, memory canon, and seed truth.

Validation is the affected bundle validation, generated catalog check, root
repository validation, and the method-growth mechanic route checks in
`scripts/validate_repo.py`.
